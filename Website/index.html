    About Us

    "Thoughout history, people have used cairns as milestones and trailmarkers to help navigate difficult paths and remind them of those who have gone on before them. Like its namesake, Cairn University bears witness to the foundational truths of scripture and sends students forward with professional competence and character."

    A Rich History

    Cairn University was founded in 1913 and enjoys a heritage that spans over one hundred years.

    A Bright Future

    Today, with over 70 academic programs and specializatons, Cairn is one of the most thoroughly accredited schools of its type.
